Who We Are

Travel With Me Expeditions is a premier adventure travel company specializing in guided hiking trips across the Philippines. Our mission is to provide unforgettable experiences by exploring the country’s most stunning landscapes.

Our Mission

At Travel With Me Expeditions, we believe in the power of exploration and adventure. Our goal is to:

  • Connect travelers with nature through immersive experiences.
  • Promote responsible and sustainable tourism.
  • Ensure safety, fun, and enrichment in every journey.

  • Forest Adventures – Hike through lush rainforests and discover unique wildlife.
  • Coastal Retreats – Experience pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters.
